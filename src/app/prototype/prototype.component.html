This is the final prototype for Samuel Whittenberger's OMS CS6750 Individual Project. This prototype is meant to showcase a more straightforward way for users to customize the appearance of captions within the Netflix video player. View the background of the task and the initial prototypes here, or the results for project surveys here.

The prototype is meant to be a semi-functional duplicate of the existing Netflix video player with the proposed features added. Access the new subtitle customization menu by opening the captions menu in the lower right hand side of the screen and selecting the gear icon next to 'Subtitles.' Captions can also be enabled/disabled by pressing 'c' on your keyboard. This information menu can be opened again by selecting the icon in the top left of the screen or by pressing 'i' on your keyboard.
